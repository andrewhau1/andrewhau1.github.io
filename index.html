About Me

Originally from Ohio, I moved to Charlotte, NC in 2008 after graduation because I wanted to live in a place with sunshine more than 3 months in a year. In my free time, I spend almost all of it with my wife and 1yr old son where I take many home videos of us together.

I have a small Youtube channel where I create and edit “glorified versions” of those home movies. Having those memories recorded and stored in a place like Youtube makes it easy for my wife and me and our families to watch these special moments in our lives with our child.
