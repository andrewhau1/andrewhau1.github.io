About Me!

Hi! My name is Andrew Au. I'm originally from Cincinnati, OH but have lived in Charlotte, NC since 2008. I am currently employed with Tech Talent South, an education startup that focuses on teaching web development in 8 weeks. We teach multiple languages such as Ruby, Ruby on Rails, Domain Modeling, HTTP and "RESTful" Design, HTML/CSS, Javascript, APIs, and everything else for our students to become a Full Stack Web Developer!

In my spare time, I become semi-obsessed over Ultimate Frisbee. I compete on Charlotte's Competitive Mixed Club Team, Charlotte Storm and coach a high school ultimate team, the Charlotte Christian Knights. The rest of my time? It goes toward a "healthy" mix of shoes, sports aesthetics, and attempting to learn to play guitar.
